## Earning Rewards, Levels & Conditions

### **Rewards and Levels for Successful Referrals**

Earn rewards based on the number of **successful referrals** as follows:

- **Level 1:** $15 reward for each of the **first 5 referrals**.

- **Level 2:** $20 reward for each referral from the **6th to the 10th referral**.

- **Level 3:** $25 reward for each referral starting from the **11th referral onward**.

### Reward Conditions

- Rewards are given only if the referred friend **registers using the referral link** and **subscribes to a Paperguide plan within 30 days**.

- **Payout Timing:** Rewards are paid on a **net-30 basis** (30 days after the referred friend subscribes to a paid plan).

- **Cookie Validity:** Referral cookies remain valid for **30 days** from the time the referral link is clicked.

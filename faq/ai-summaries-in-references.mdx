---
title: How do I get AI Summaries of papers in my references?
description: >-
  How to generate AI summaries for papers saved in your Paperguide Reference
  Manager.
---
Paperguide provides concise AI-generated summaries of your selected papers, including key concepts, findings, and more.

[https://www.loom.com/embed/2eb8120497124ce6a0c7871f5132fe22?sid=a4a10205-25e9-4c35-9471-8f29c9d2f17c](https://www.loom.com/embed/2eb8120497124ce6a0c7871f5132fe22?sid=a4a10205-25e9-4c35-9471-8f29c9d2f17c)

1. **Select a Paper from Reference Manager**

   In your Paperguide Reference Manager, click on the paper you want summarized. Once clicked, a panel will open on the right side of the screen with the **Bib** tab selected by default.

2. **Switch to AI Summary Tab**

   In the right-side panel, switch to the **AI Summary** tab.

3. **Generate AI Summary**

   Click on the Summary tab **Generate** button to get a detailed AI summary of the paper. The summary will include:

   - **Key Concepts**

     The main ideas, theories, or terms that are essential to understanding the study's focus, context, or findings.

   - **Highlights**

     Highlights provide a quick overview of the most significant findings or insights of a paper, typically in bullet points.

   - **Synopsis**

     A quick overview of the research, meant to give a general understanding without much detail, for casual readering.

   - **Summary**

     It provides a condensed version of the entire paper, including the background, methods, results, discussion, and conclusion.

4. **Review the Summary**

   Once the summary is generated, you can review it for a quick understanding of the paper without reading the full text.

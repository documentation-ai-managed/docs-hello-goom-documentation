---
title: How can I share my folders/papers in the reference manager?
---
Paperguide's Reference Manager allows you to share folders and collaborate with others by assigning specific roles and permissions.

<Video uid="09534d2e-a70a-45d9-897d-a9bb51a879bc" src="https://www.loom.com/embed/127723321ecc4342bdb9661d50af7369?sid=3a9686f9-fa43-4843-acdd-46b6eba40c34" render-type="iframe" width="672" height="378" controls="true" allow-full-screen="true" style="width: 672px; height: auto; cursor: pointer;" />

1. **Open the Reference Manager**:

   - From the dashboard, navigate to the **References** menu.

2. **Select the Folder**:

   - Choose the folder you want to share from your list of folders.

3. **Click on Share Folder**:

   - Click on theÂ **three-dot icon** next to the folder name.

   - Then, click on **Share Folder** button from the dropdown list.

4. **Enter Email Addresses**:

   - In the share window, you can invite members by entering their email addresses.

   - You can also invite non-Paperguide users to access the shared folder. Once they receive the invitation, they can easily register for Paperguide to view or access the shared folder.

5. **Assign Permissions**:

   Choose the appropriate permission for each member:

   - **Can View**: Allows the member to **only view** the folder and its papers along with notes.

   - **Can Add Notes**: Grants viewing access and the ability to **add notes** to the papers.

   - **Can Edit**: Full access to **view, edit, add notes, and modify** papers in the folder.

6. **Send Invitation**:

   - After assigning permissions, click **Send Invite**. The invited members will receive an email notification with a link to access the folder.

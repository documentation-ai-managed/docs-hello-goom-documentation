Paperguide's **AI Search** feature allows you to ask research questions and instantly receive accurate answers along with relevant academic papers, making your research faster and more efficient.

<Iframe src="https://www.loom.com/embed/70e0b43e824842249dc078f529576d83?sid=0013c1a5-6ebd-40b8-bf56-46637f8dadf3" title="How to ask questions on Public Research Database" width="672" height="378"></Iframe>

1. From the Home page, select the **AI Search** tab.
2. Ask your reseaarch related question in the AI Search tab

![image.png](image%201.png)

### Answer and References

- Paperguide will search through the 200million+ research database and generate answer backed by research papers to your question.
- Along with the answer, you'll receive a list of the **top relevant papers** used to generate the response, shown in the **References** section.
- Click on Citations to view paper and create a new chat with paper

![image.png](image%202.png)

This feature simplifies the process of finding the most relevant research papers, allowing you to focus on understanding and analyzing the information with ease.

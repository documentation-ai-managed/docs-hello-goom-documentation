---
title: How can I chat with multiple (up to 100) papers?
---
Paperguide’s AI-powered **Chat with PDF/Paper** tool allows you to chat with multiple documents to quickly gain insights and understand complex research materials.

**Here’s how to make the most of it:**

1. Once your papers/documents are added to References. Click on “**My References**” in **Chat with PDF** widget.



2. Then, select the papers/documents you want to chat. (max 100)

   - You can use folders and tags to filter and select the documents.

   - Only papers/documents with PDF attachment will be available for chat.

3. And click on “**Proceed**” button to initiate chat.

This setup allows for seamless switching between files, enabling an enriched and comprehensive research experience.

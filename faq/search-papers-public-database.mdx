# How do I search papers from Public Research Database?

With Paperguide you can discover papers for your **research question or topic** using [AI Search](AI%20Search%20111980d21a2d80739968d147b1ff3784.md) and [Literature Review](Literature%20Review%20111980d21a2d80929f3ce74c1df79090.md)

Paperguide allows you to search papers with two features. They are:

# Searching Papers with AI Search

In **AI Search**, simply ask a question, and our system will do the heavy lifting by searching for the most relevant papers on your topic. We analyze these papers and provide a clear, concise answer to your question, along with the references used to generate the response.

1. Go to AI Search tab on the Home page
2. Ask a research question or enter research topic

![image.png](image%203.png)

- Paperguide will search through a research database of 200 million+ papers and find out the most relevant papers for your topic and answer the question.

![image.png](image%204.png)

- Along with the answer, you'll receive a list of the **top relevant papers** in the **References** section.

![image.png](image%205.png)

- Add the papers into reference manager by clicking **References** button below the paper title or chat with them (if they have pdf available)
- Use Filters to refine the research further both in literature review section and AI search section

![image.png](image%206.png)

Learn more about [AI Search](AI%20Search%20111980d21a2d80739968d147b1ff3784.md) 

# Searching Papers with Literature Review

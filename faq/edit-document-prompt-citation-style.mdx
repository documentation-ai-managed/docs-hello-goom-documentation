# How do I edit my document prompt or citation style?

You can edit your prompt and citation style at any time in your writer document settings.

![image.png](image%2012.png)

- To edit document settings or prompt, select the "Gear" icon in the top right side of the Writer screen.
- From here, you can edit/update your **document prompt** and get suggetions based on updated prompts.
    
    ![image.png](image%2013.png)
    
- Below document prompt, you can access writer document **citation style**. You can update it anytime and all your citations in your writer document automatically updates according to the citation style selected.
    
    ![image.png](image%2014.png)

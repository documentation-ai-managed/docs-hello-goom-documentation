---
title: How do I edit my document prompt or citation style?
---
You can edit your prompt and citation style at any time in your writer document settings.

<Image uid="de216760-234d-4b9f-bf3b-c174671bafa3" src="https://blob-cdn.documentation.ai/org-32657b98-c10c-411d-8b88-f43a8010b91d/doc-f333a884-cde9-44eb-8107-3714d598a727/1764756881639-00dzoipzebxem-pasted-image-1764756880274.png" width="739" height="205" alt="" />

- To edit document settings or prompt, select the "Gear" icon in the top right side of the Writer screen.

- From here, you can edit/update your **document prompt** and get suggetions based on updated prompts.



- Below document prompt, you can access writer document **citation style**. You can update it anytime and all your citations in your writer document automatically update according to the citation style selected.

## AI Humanizer: Making Text Sound Natural

The AI Humanizer enhances your text to ensure it sounds natural and engaging, akin to being written by a human. This tool refines the tone, style, and structure for better reader engagement.

## How It Works

This feature adjusts:

- **Tone**: Tailors the emotional tone to your audience, ensuring appropriateness and engagement.

- **Vocabulary**: Employs everyday phrases and synonyms to enrich your text.

- **Sentence Structure**: Utilizes varied sentence lengths to replicate natural speech patterns.

## Usage

Enable the AI Humanizer by toggling it in your settings. It's perfect for enhancing marketing content, customer communications, and technical documentation.

## AI Bypass

Primary objective of AI Humanizer is generate natural sounding text and also any remove filler words typically AI generates. This might not bypass AI detectors as of now but we are continuously working on improving the bypass logic without compromising on the context.

---
title: Share Folders in My References
---
Paperguide's Reference Manager allows you to share folders and collaborate with others by assigning specific roles and permissions.

## Create and Share Folder

1. **Open the Reference Manager**:

   - From the dashboard, navigate to the **References** menu.

2. **Select the Folder**:

   - Choose the folder you want to share from your list of folders.

3. **Click on Share Folder**:

   - Click on the **three-dot icon** next to the folder name.

   - Then, click on **the Share Folder** button from the dropdown list.

4. **Enter Email Addresses**:

   - In the share window, you can invite members by entering their email addresses.

   - You can also invite non-Paperguide users to access the shared folder. Once they receive the invitation, they can easily register for Paperguide to view or access the shared folder.

5. **Assign Permissions**:

   Choose the appropriate permission for each member:

   - **Can View**: Allows the member to **only view** the folder and its papers/documents along with notes.

   - **Can Add Notes**: Grants viewing access and the ability to **add notes** to the papers/documents.

   - **Can Edit**: Full access to **view, edit, add notes, and modify** papers/documents in the folder.

6. **Send Invitation**:

   - After assigning permissions, click **Send Invite**. The invited members will receive an email notification with a link to access the folder.

## Manage Shared Folders

1. **View Shared Folder Members**:

   - Click on the **three-dot icon** next to the folder name.

   - Then, click on the **Manage Members** button from the dropdown list.

2. **Edit Member Roles**:
   Next to each member's name, you can adjust their role. Change the appropriate permission for each member based on your needs:

   - **Can View**: Allows the member to **only view** the folder and its papers/documents along with notes.

   - **Can Add Notes**: Grants viewing access and the ability to **add notes** to the papers/documents.

   - **Can Edit**: Full access to **view, edit, add notes, and modify** papers/documents in the folder.

3. **Remove a Member**:

   - If you no longer want a member to have access to the folder, you can click **Remove** (delete icon) on the right side of their name. They will no longer be able to access the shared folder.

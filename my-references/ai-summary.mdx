## How It Works

The AI Summary feature can be used on any paper you upload or import into Paperguide. With one click, Paperguide processes the document and displays a succinct summary that highlights objectives, methodology, results, and conclusions.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Upload or Import Your Paper" icon="upload" title-type="p">
    Select a PDF or supported file from your computer, or import one from a reference manager (such as Zotero).
  </Step>

  <Step title="Generate AI Summary" icon="SparklesIcon" title-type="p">
    Click the "Summarize with AI" button next to your paper reference. The system analyzes the entire paper using natural language processing.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Review the Structured Summary" icon="list" title-type="p">
    The summary will appear directly within your reference details. It highlights:

    - Main objectives and research questions

    - Methods and experimental design

    - Key results and findings

    - Primary conclusions
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Important Verification Note

<ExpandableGroup>
  <Expandable title="What types of papers are best suited for AI summarization?" default-open="false">
    Most peer-reviewed journals, conference papers, and preprints in PDF format are supported. Papers with clear structure (abstract, sections, references) yield the best summaries.
  </Expandable>

  <Expandable title="What information is included or excluded in the AI summary?" default-open="false">
    Summaries emphasize objectives, methods, principal findings, and main conclusions. Supplementary data, figures, or precise statistical values may not be referenced explicitly.
  </Expandable>
</ExpandableGroup>

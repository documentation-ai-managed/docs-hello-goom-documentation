---
title: Import from Zotero
---
Paperguide’s Zotero integration helps you bring your library into the Paperguide reference manager. To start importing from Zotero, first you need to connect your Zotero account.

## Connect your Zotero account to Paperguide

1. You can connect your Zotero account by navigating to your **profile.&#x20;**&#x54;hen, go to Integrations.

2. Or click on **Add Papers** and select **Import from Zotero**, then click on connect.

3. On clicking connect, you are redirected to the **Zotero Web Page** asking to **Accept Defaults** for Paperguide.

4. Once you accept defaults, you are all set for importing papers from Zotero.

<Callout uid="e2bc72cb-37f2-4e1f-8d89-5b2ea51d4525" kind="alert" collapsed="false">
  You can disconnect your Zotero account from **the Profile > Integrations** section. To reconnect the Zotero account, repeat the same connecting process.
</Callout>

## Importing from Zotero

1. Once you have connected your Zotero account with Paperguide, click on **Add Papers** and select **Import from Zotero.**

2. You’ll see a popup displaying your Zotero collection. Select the folders you want to import and click on the Import button.

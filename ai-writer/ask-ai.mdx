title: Start with AI Writer's Ask AI

Paperguide's **Ask AI** feature allows you to generate content in your document with AI assistance. It provides various writing options, such as continuing a section, drafting an introduction, or writing with more depth.

## Generate content using Ask AI

<Video uid="9f32ce26-6233-4b2f-93f5-bc6368e9f9c1" src="https://www.loom.com/embed/b164dd4c549545e88d4ace34113e8817" render-type="iframe" width="560" height="315" controls="true" allow-full-screen="true" />

Here's a step-by-step guide to using this feature:

- **Open 'Ask AI'**:

  - Go to the new line where you want to generate content using AI.

  - At the bottom of the writing area, you will find the âœ¨ **Ask AI** button; click on it or press **Ctrl+J** to open the AI suggestions dropdown menu.

- **Choose a Writing Option**:

  From the dropdown menu, you will see several AI-powered writing options:

  - **Custom Prompt:** In the input field displayed after opening Ask AI, you can enter your own custom prompt to generate your writing exactly how you need it. Or you can choose any of the template options below:

  - **Continue Writing**: The AI will help you continue from where you left off.

  - **Write an Introduction**: Generates a well-structured introduction based on your content.

  - **Write a Conclusion**: Helps you craft a concluding paragraph that summarizes your arguments.

  - **Write an Opposing Argument**: If you need to present a counterargument, this option helps you frame it.

  - **Write with More Depth**: Expands on your existing content by providing more details or depth to a section.

- **Edit and Refine**:

  - After selecting the appropriate suggestion, the AI will generate content. You can review and refine the text to better fit your writing style or adjust the argumentation.

- **Continue Working**:

  - Once you have used the suggestion, you can continue writing or use **Ask AI** again to assist with other sections of your document.

### **Tips for Using Ask AI Suggestions**

- **Tailor Suggestions to Your Needs**: Use the option that fits the stage of your writing. For example, use **Write with More Depth** when you need to elaborate on a point.

- **Mix and Match**: You can combine different AI suggestions, like writing an introduction and then asking AI to **continue writing.**

- **Refine AI Output**: AI suggestions are a starting point. Always review and edit the content to ensure it aligns with your ideas and tone.

## Editing content using Ask AI

<Video uid="917be93a-e9f3-4eb8-a02c-ab275d2cfaba" src="https://www.loom.com/embed/70f8ea2726e74a1b9d8b90ed68b827cd" render-type="iframe" width="672" height="378" controls="true" allow-full-screen="true" style="width: 672px; height: auto; cursor: pointer;" />

Here's a step-by-step guide to using this feature:

At any time, you can **highlight** a section of **text** from Paperguide and edit it using Ask AI Commands. You can ask Paperguide to edit using a custom prompt or a complete set of preset functions:

- **Custom Prompt:** In the input field you can enter your own custom prompt to edit or modify your writing exactly how you need.

- **Improve Writing**: Refines your writing by enhancing readability, flow, and style.

- **Humanize (AI Bypass)**: Adjusts the text to make it sound more natural and less AI-generated.

- **Change Tone**: Adjusts the tone of the writing as you need.

- **Simplify**: Reduces complexity in the text, making it easier to read and understand.

- **Translate**: Allows you to translate the selected text into various languages for multilingual support.

1. Once the text is selected, a toolbar with a few options will be displayed along the text selection.

2. Click on the **Ask AI** on the toolbar. Then enter a custom prompt in the input field or select the **Edit** command you want from the dropdown.

3. Once Paperguide has generated your edited version, you will have the option to accept it for your paper, try another suggestion, or delete it and try again with a different edit input.

<Callout uid="777ac14d-b3ff-4971-b4e9-38b675d1426b" kind="info" collapsed="false">
  **Example:** Choose AI commands based on the context of your writing. For example, use **Change Tone** when switching from academic writing to a more informal blog style.
</Callout>

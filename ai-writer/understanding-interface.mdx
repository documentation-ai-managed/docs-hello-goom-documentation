Once the document is created, the Paperguide Writer provides several key features:

## **Elements in AI Writer**

This is where you will write and format your document.

### **Formatting Tools**

To open text formatting options, type “/” in the new line. It includes Text, Headings, Bullet & Numbered lists, blockquotes, to-do lists, horizontal rules, code blocks, etc.&#x20;

You can even select/highlight text and change formatting options.

### **Ask AI**

To open the AI command bar and options, use the shortcut “**Ctrl + J**”, or click on “**Ask AI**” on the bottom toolbar, or select/highlight text and select “Ask AI” from the toolbar.

### **Cite**

To cite a paper or open the citation manager, type “@” in the doc where you want to add the citation, or click on the “Cite” button on the bottom toolbar.

### Header

Options/tools are provided in the header space for quick access.

- **Reference Manager**: You can seamlessly pull in references from your **references** to cite and read/view papers/references as you write.

- **Check Plagiarism:** To scan your content across millions of sources, giving you instant feedback to refine your writing with confidence.

- **Settings:** To customize your **document topic, citation style, language, and more** for the document.

- **Export:** To export your document.

### Outline

Writer allows you to organize and visualize the structure of your document at a glance.

To view the “**outline/table of contents**” of your writer document, hover on the lines (which represent the outline) on the left side of your document.

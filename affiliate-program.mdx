## Terms & Conditions

<Expandable title="Is there any limit on the payout?" default-open="false">
  No, there is no limit on the payout. You can earn unlimited commissions based on the sales generated through your affiliate links. The more you promote and sell, the more you can earn!
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How long does the cookie last?" default-open="false">
  The cookie lasts for 30 days. This means that if a user clicks on your affiliate link and makes a purchase within 30 days, you will receive a commission for that sale.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How can I track my sales and earnings?" default-open="false">
  You can track your sales and earnings through your affiliate dashboard available at [<a href="https://affiliates.paperguide.ai/" target="_blank" rel="noopener noreferrer">https://affiliates.paperguide.ai/</a>](https://affiliates.paperguide.ai/). Once you log in, you will have access to detailed reports showing your clicks, conversions, and commissions earned. This allows you to monitor your performance and optimize your marketing strategies.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="How will I receive my commissions?" default-open="false">
  Commissions are paid out via your chosen payment method. We offer multiple payment options, including PayPal, bank transfer, and other popular payment methods. You can select your preferred method in your affiliate account settings.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="When do I get paid?" default-open="false">
  Payments are made on a monthly basis. Commissions earned in a given month will be paid out by the 30th of the following month. For example, commissions earned in January will be paid by February 28th. Please ensure that your payment details are up to date to avoid any delays.
</Expandable>

<Expandable title="Can I run paid ads?" default-open="false">
  No, affiliates cannot use the brand name, logo, or trademarks in any paid ads or campaigns without prior approval. Violating this guideline will result in no commission for any sales made through such ads.
</Expandable>

title: Writing your first document
---
Paperguide’s **Writer** feature is designed to streamline your academic writing process, offering AI-powered tools to help you draft, edit, and refine your research papers, project reports, essays, or blog articles.

Follow these simple steps to create your first document and take full advantage of our AI-powered writing support:

## **1. Create a New Document**

From the left-hand panel, click the **+ New Document** button. This opens a blank workspace for your writing.

## 2. Set Up Document Settings

In the right-hand panel, you'll see the **Document Settings** section. Here's what you need to do:&#x20;

Choose the most appropriate format for your writing: Research Paper, Literature Review, Case Study, Article, and Essay

## 3. Describe the Topic and Provide Additional details

Provide a detailed overview of your writing **topic or research question**. Include the subject, desired word count, and any optional preferences such as tone or writing style.

➡️ The more detailed and specific your prompt, the better the AI will understand the structure and topic of your document, leading to more accurate suggestions and a tailored outline.

## 4. Connect Knowledge Sources

You can now fine-tune the **Ask AI** suggestions in the Writer by selecting specific **references** or **folders** from your reference manager or selecting a public research database to get suggestions from the papers available in public sources.

This gives you more control over the sources used, making your AI-generated content more accurate, focused, and relevant to your topic.

## 5. Start Writing or Let AI Help You Begin

Now you’re all set! You can start writing manually, or click **Generate Outline** to let AI create a structured outline based on your topic and selected document type.

Click Ask AI anytime in the toolbar to get real-time support and ideas—AI[ Suggestions and Commands](https://www.notion.so/AI-Suggestions-and-Commands-112980d21a2d8079b0d9f2373810d287?pvs=21)

✨ Want the AI to take the first step for you? Click [AI Generate Full Document](https://www.notion.so/AI-Generate-Full-Document-1c9980d21a2d80b1ac35c23fb5b29a1d?pvs=21) to instantly create a **complete first draft** based on your inputs.

Perfect for jumpstarting your writing or overcoming writer’s block!

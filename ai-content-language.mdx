## Setting the Default AI Generation Language

1. **Navigate to Settings**: Open your Paperguide settings by clicking on your profile.

2. **Select General Settings**: Find the "General" section within the settings screen.

3. **Change AI Generation Language**: Click on "AI Generation Language" and select your preferred language from the dropdown menu.

### Paperguide currently supports the following languages:

- **English (US and UK)**

- **German**

- **French**

- **Spanish**

- **Portuguese (EU and BR)**

- **Italian**

- **Japanese**

- **Chinese (Mandarin)**

- **Indonesian**

- **Thai**

- **Vietnamese**

- **Others**

<Callout uid="cbe27851-e4c2-4dfe-a811-99b729f3d609" kind="tip" collapsed="false">
  **For unsupported languages:** If you're using Chat with PDF and want to ask a question (or) Writer to respond in Russian (example), you could enter a prompt: *Explain (your question) in Russian?*
</Callout>

## Feature-Specific Language Settings

### **Writer**

- Content generated via "**Ask AI**" commands will be in the selected default language.

- To change the language for an individual document, go to '**Document Settings**' within the Writer.

<Callout uid="7a68e789-549f-4264-b0ff-95532ac0cd83" kind="info" collapsed="false">
  If your writing language isn't available, select "Others." AI will generate content in English, and you can use commands like "Write an introduction in \[language]."
</Callout>

### **Chat with PDF**

- AI responses and answers will be in the default language.

- To change the language for an individual chat, use the language option at the top right corner of the chat window.

### **Workbooks (Literature Review and Extract Data)**:

- AI will generate answers and data columns in the selected language.

- To adjust the language for individual entries, use the language option at the top right of your workbook screen.

By setting your preferred language, you can tailor the AI's output to better fit your linguistic needs, enhancing your workflow in Paperguide.

title: Building My References (Reference Manager)
---
Paperguide offers a number of different ways to upload papers/references into your reference manager. Every research paper you upload will have its bib and citation automatically generated.

**To upload a reference into Paperguide Reference Manager:**

<Callout uid="459d9a6e-4256-481a-8fe4-f0477e2e0932" kind="info" collapsed="false">
  Go to [My References](https://paperguide.ai/references/) and click on the "**Add Papers**" button. You'll see several options for adding papers/documents.
</Callout>

## Upload URL/DOI

1. Select **Upload URL/DOI** from the dropdown.

2. Paste the paper’s **URL or DOI** (journal, book, article, etc.) and it auto-fetches metadata and open-access PDFs.

<Callout uid="7edfc3fe-5b93-450c-ab1c-ddecd7a7607f" kind="info" collapsed="false">
  For best results, add references using URLs or DOIs. Our system will automatically retrieve the metadata and download the PDF if it's available as open access.
</Callout>

## Search Papers Online

- Choose **Search Papers Online** from the dropdown. This will open the search interface for accessing the research database.

- In the search bar, enter relevant keywords, or paper titles to search papers.

- Browse the list of papers that match your search, and click **Add Paper** button to import.

*Even here, Paperguide auto-fetches metadata and open-access PDFs.*

## Upload Files

Paperguide allows you to upload files directly into your Reference Manager for easy organization and reference management.

- Choose **Upload File** from the dropdown, and click on **Select** to open file selection.

- Select up to 30 PDF or supported files at once from your computer.

- After selecting the files, Paperguide will automatically extract metadata for the uploaded research papers.

## Import BibTeX or RIS

Paperguide supports importing references through **BibTeX** and **RIS** files, making it easy to manage your references. You can either upload files directly or paste the BibTeX/RIS text. Follow these steps:

### Import via BibTeX/RIS Text

- Choose **BibTeX/RIS** from the dropdown.

- Paste your BibTeX or RIS text in the popup.

- Click **Import** to add to your library.

<Callout uid="f03592bf-c4f5-4094-b612-83c042139c4f" kind="info" collapsed="false">
  * Ensure that your BibTeX/RIS files or text are formatted correctly for smooth importing.

  * You can upload or paste multiple references at once, making it easier to manage large citation lists.
</Callout>

### Import via BibTeX/RIS File Upload

- Switch to **Upload File** tab.

- Select `.bib` or `.ris` files from your computer.

## Add Manually

In addition, Paperguide allows you to manually add references by entering all the necessary citation details. Follow these steps to add references manually:

- Click the **Add Papers** button at the top of the Reference Manager. From the dropdown menu, select **Add Manually**.

- A form will appear where you can fill in all relevant citation details (title, authors, publication date, etc.)

- Complete as many fields as possible to ensure accurate citation data.

- Once all the details are entered, click the **Save** button to add the reference to your library.

<Callout uid="d1b15a8d-e7ee-4527-95bf-c018a99b779f" kind="tip" collapsed="false">
  Make sure to enter all available citation information to ensure the reference is complete and properly formatted.
</Callout>

# How can I add Papers to my reference manager?

Paperguide offers a number of different ways to upload papers into your reference manager. Every paper you upload will have its bib and citation automatically generated.

To upload a paper into Paperguide Reference Manager, Click on the "**Add Papers**" button. You'll see several options for adding papers.

# Z - Import from Zotero

Paperguide‚Äôs Zotero integration helps you bring your library into paperguide reference manager. To start importing from Zotero, first you need to connect your Zotero account.

### **Connect your Zotero account to Papeguide**

1. You can connect your Zotero account by navigating to your **Profile.** Then, go to Intergrations.
2. Or Click on **Add Papers** and Select **Import from Zotero**, then click on connect.
3. On clicking connect, you are redirected to **Zotero Web Page** asking to **Accept Defaults** for Paperguide.
4. Once you accept defaults, you are all set for importing papers from Zotero.

You can disconnect your Zotero account from **Profile > Intergrations** Section. To reconnect Zotero account, repeat the same connecting process.

### Importing from Zotero

1. Once you have conencted your Zotero account with Paperguide, click on **Add Papers** and Select **Import from Zotero.**
2. You‚Äôll see a popup displying your Zotero collection. Select the folders you want to import and click on Import button.

# üìÑ Upload Files

Paperguide allows you to upload files directly into your Reference Manager for easy organization and reference management. Follow these steps to import papers via the **Upload Files** option:

- From the **Add Papers‚Äô** dropdown menu, select **Upload File**. This will open a file upload dialog box.
- Browse your computer to select the PDF files or other supported formats of the papers you want to upload. You can select one or multiple (upto 10) files at a time.
- After selecting the files, click the **Upload** button. Paperguide will automatically extract relevant metadata (title, author, publication details) from the uploaded files and store them in your reference library.

# üóíÔ∏è Import BibTeX or RIS

Paperguide supports importing references through **BibTeX** and **RIS** files, making it easy to manage your references. You can either upload files directly or paste the BibTeX/RIS text. Follow these steps:

### Import via BibTeX/RIS Text

[https://www.loom.com/embed/b3ab7c33c27a4792bfc95da3cf280671?sid=3d30cb3e-1e40-465a-bc56-0aac94410f21](https://www.loom.com/embed/b3ab7c33c27a4792bfc95da3cf280671?sid=3d30cb3e-1e40-465a-bc56-0aac94410f21)

- Click the **Add Papers** button at the top of the Reference Manager and choose the **BibTeX/RIS** option.
- In the modal that appears, you will see two tabs. By default, the **Paste Text** tab will be selected. Here, you can copy and paste the BibTeX or RIS text directly.
- Copy the BibTeX or RIS formatted text from your source and paste it into the provided text box and click the **Import** button.

**Tips**

- Ensure that your BibTeX/RIS files or text are formatted correctly for smooth importing.
- You can upload or paste multiple references at once, making it easier to manage large citation lists.

# ‚úçÔ∏è Add Manually

In addition, Paperguide allows you to manually add references by entering all the necessary citation details. Follow these steps to add references manually:

- Click the **Add Papers** button at the top of the Reference Manager. From the dropdown menu, select **Add Manually**.
- A form will appear where you can fill in all relevant citation details (title, authors, publication date, etc.)
- Complete as many fields as possible to ensure accurate citation data.
- Once all the details are entered, click the **Save** button to add the reference to your library.

**Tips**

- Make sure to enter all available citation information to ensure the reference is complete and properly formatted.

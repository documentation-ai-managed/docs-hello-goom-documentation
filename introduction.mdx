## Benefits

Paperguide reduces time spent searching, organising, and writing so you can focus on understanding and insight.

- Faster literature reviews with AI-generated summaries and deep research reports.

- Smarter writing support with AI-assisted drafting and integrated citations.

- Centralised reference management with folders, tags, notes, and annotations.

- Clear, research-backed answers pulled directly from high-quality papers.

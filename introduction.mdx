## **What you can do with Paperguide**

Paperguide brings search, reading, note-taking, and writing into a single workspace so you can move from idea to finished draft faster.

- **AI Search**: Find relevant, high‑quality papers and research‑backed answers.

- **Deep Research Reports**: Generate structured multi‑paper reports with key findings and themes.

- **Chat with Papers**: Ask questions, clarify methods, and analyze specific sections of any paper.

- **My References**: Save papers from AI Search, import libraries (RIS, BibTeX, files, and Zotero), and organize with folders and tags.

- **AI Summaries**: Get quick, accurate summaries of individual papers or collections.

- **Notes & Annotations**: Highlight, comment, and keep organized notes directly on your papers.

- **AI Writer**: Draft, refine, and edit documents with AI assistance.

- **Citations & References**: Insert citations and build reference lists automatically from your library.

- **Workbooks (Literature Review & Extract Data)**: Systematically review, compare, and extract key data from multiple papers.

- **Collaboration & Sharing**: Share folders and papers with others and manage access to your reference collections.

## Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find Papers and Research-backed Answers" icon="search" title-type="p">
    Enter your research question and explore AI-curated results and summaries.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add to My References" icon="upload" title-type="p">
    Save papers from AI Search to your reference library, and import existing sources via RIS, BibTeX, file upload, or Zotero so everything stays in one place.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Chat with Your References" icon="message-circle" title-type="p">
    In My References, select papers, click on Chat with Papers, and ask questions to understand methods, results, and key insights.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Write with AI Assistance" icon="file-text" title-type="p">
    Draft sections with the AI Writer while automatically pulling citations and evidence from your references.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Benefits

Paperguide reduces time spent searching, organizing, and writing so you can focus on understanding and insight.

- Faster literature reviews with AI-generated summaries and deep research reports.

- Smarter writing support with AI-assisted drafting and integrated citations.

- Centralized reference management with folders, tags, notes, and annotations.

- Clear, research-backed answers pulled directly from high-quality papers.

## Smart Discovery

You discover relevant literature effortlessly with Paperguide's AI search engine. It processes queries in natural language, returning results from vast academic databases. The system ranks items by novelty, citation impact, and alignment with your research goals.

<Columns cols={3}>
  <Card title="Contextual Search" icon="search" href="#">
    Understands query intent beyond keywords.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Related Works" icon="link" href="#">
    Suggests interconnected papers automatically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Trend Analysis" icon="trending-up" href="#">
    Identifies emerging topics in your field.
  </Card>
</Columns>

````javascript
// Performing a contextual search
const results = await paperguide.discover({
  query: 'sustainable energy solutions {renewable}' ,
  context: 'policy implications',
  maxResults: 25
});
````

## Intelligent Analysis

You analyze documents with AI tools that extract insights, detect biases, and summarize content. Upload papers, and Paperguide generates mind maps of concepts and relationships.

<Callout kind="info">
  Analysis runs on secure servers; results are private to your account.
</Callout>

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Summary Generation" icon="file-text">
    Get concise overviews of long papers.
    
````python
analysis = client.analyze(document_id='doc123', focus='key findings');
print(analysis.summary);
````
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Entity Extraction" icon="cpu">
    Identifies people, organizations, and methods mentioned in text.
    
````javascript
const entities = await client.extractEntities({
  text: 'The study by Smith et al. uses neural networks.'
});
// Returns: [{type: 'person', name: 'Smith'}]
````
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Unified Management

You manage your entire research library in one place. Features include duplicate detection, version tracking, and collaborative folders.

  <Expandable title="Collaboration Tools">

  </Expandable>
  <Expandable title="Export Options">

  </Expandable>

| Auto-Deduplication | Merges similar entries | Saves storage and time |
| Search Within Library | Full-text indexing | Quick retrieval `>90% accuracy` |

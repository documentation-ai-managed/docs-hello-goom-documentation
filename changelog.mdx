<Update label="12 May, 2025" tags={["feature"]}>
  ## **Deep Research Report**

  Deep Research is our new research assistant that automatically conducts comprehensive literature reviews for you. Here's what it does:

  - Create a structured research plan

  - Search through millions of papers for relevant information

  - Screen papers based on quality and relevance

  - Extract key data points and identify patterns

  - Deliver a fully cited research report

  This can help you complete in minutes what might normally take weeks of manual work.
</Update>

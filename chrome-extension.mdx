## **Core Features**

### Add Papers to Your Reference Manager From Anywhere

Collect research instantly—no copying, downloading, or manual entry required.

- While viewing a research article or webpage, simply open the extension and click **“Add to My References.”**

- Choose a folder to organize exactly where the reference should go.

- Paperguide automatically:

  - Detects and extracts the full-text PDF (when available)

  - Captures the webpage’s HTML version for non-PDF sources

  - Retrieves and saves citation and bibliographic metadata

- Works across journals, publisher sites, research repositories, blogs, and news sources.

Spend time analyzing—not formatting references.

Generate and copy citations without leaving your writing environment.

- Use the built-in search bar to instantly find references in your library.

- Copy formatted citations with one click.

- Paste directly into documents, emails, slides, or note-taking apps.

### AI Chat With Any Webpage or Research Paper

Understand, summarize, or question articles instantly—right where you're reading.

- Open the extension and select **“Ask AI.”**

- Start chatting with the paper or webpage currently open in your browser.

- Ask for summaries, definitions, methodology explanations, comparisons, insights, and more.

Turn complex research into conversations—no switching tabs or copy-pasting.

### Smart Action Buttons on Research Databases & Publisher Sites

Paperguide integrates directly into the platforms you already use.

- Custom action buttons appear on popular scholarly websites.

- Create references or add papers to collections with a single click.

- Skip downloads, forms, and external tools—Paperguide handles it automatically.

Your reference manager follows you across the research web.
